# PIZZA-SALES-ANALYSIS
SQL analysis of pizza sales data to identify sales trends, top-performing products, and business insights.

## Executive Summary

This project analyzed pizza sales data using SQL to uncover sales trends, customer preferences, and revenue drivers.

### Key Findings

* Total Revenue: $817,860.05
* Total Orders: 21,350
* Total Pizzas Sold: 49,754
* Average Order Value: $38.31
* Best-Selling Pizza: Classic Deluxe Pizza
* Most Popular Size: Large (18,956 pizzas sold)
* Highest Revenue Category: Classic ($220,053.10)

### Business Insights

The Classic category generated the highest revenue, contributing over $220,000 in sales. The Classic Deluxe Pizza was the most popular individual pizza, indicating strong customer preference for traditional menu options.

Large pizzas were the most frequently purchased size, suggesting that customers often order for families or groups. With an average order value of $38.31, customers typically purchase multiple items per transaction.

### Recommendations

* Promote top-performing Classic pizzas through targeted marketing campaigns.
* Ensure sufficient inventory for large-sized pizzas during peak demand periods.
* Bundle best-selling products to increase average order value.
* Analyze peak ordering times to optimize staffing and operational efficiency.
